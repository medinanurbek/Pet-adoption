The Steps

1. Browse pets

Find your ideal pet on our Adopt page.

2. Submit application

Fill out a form so we can match you with the right companion.

3. Meet & greet

Schedule a visit at the shelter or online.

4. Home check

Ensure your home is safe and cozy for the pet.

5. Adopt & love

Sign agreement, pay fee (if any), and welcome your pet!

Frequently Asked Questions

Pet adoption is the process of taking a pet from a shelter or rescue center and providing it with a permanent home.

Adopting a pet gives them a second chance and provides you with a loyal companion.

You can browse available pets on our website, fill out an adoption application, and schedule a visit.

The adoption process includes browsing pets, submitting an application, meeting the pet, and completing the adoption paperwork.
